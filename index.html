Zachariah Micallef is presently undergoing his Bachelor of Arts (Photography) at RMIT University. He currently lives and works in Melbourne (Naarm). His artistic work revolves around the intersection of photography, and sculpture, interrogating the methods of representation which manifest when imaging non-human objects. With the help of object-oriented ontology, he creates bodies of work which deconstruct the mechanisms of photography, allowing non-human objects agency in their own representations.

Aside from his own artistic practice, Zachariah Micallef works freelance developing publication and front-end website design for various clients.
